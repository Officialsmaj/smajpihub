Cookies Policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains how SMAJ PI HUB uses cookies and similar technologies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to improve your experience, remember preferences, and provide analytics. They help us deliver better services and personalize content for you.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function, including login, cart, and payment features.
  • Performance Cookies: Track usage patterns to improve the website and services.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences such as language and theme.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand traffic, trends, and user behavior for better decision-making.
  • Third-party Cookies: May be used by payment providers, social media widgets, and analytics services.

Why We Use Cookies

Cookies allow SMAJ PI HUB to provide:

  • Secure logins and transactions using Pi Wallet.
  • Personalized recommendations and content based on your activity.
  • Analytics and performance tracking to improve user experience.
  • Marketing insights to provide relevant offers and promotions.

Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies via your browser settings. Note that some functionalities of SMAJ PI HUB may not work if cookies are disabled.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information about the cookies used.
  • Request deletion of personal data collected through cookies.
  • Refuse non-essential cookies without affecting essential functionalities.

Policy Updates

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available here. Continued use of SMAJ PI HUB indicates acceptance of updated policies.
